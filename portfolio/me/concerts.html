    • Short Message

    One of my favorite things about life is going to concerts and music festivals! I believe the experience of each one is truly significant and irreplaceable, which is why I tend to invest my time and money into it. If the price of the tickets are decent and justifiable, I will take up the opportunity to go! I listen to all kinds of music and I enjoy discovering new artists!

    All the concerts will be listed in chronological order below!!

    BTS - Love Yourself Tour

    September 2018, Los Angeles

    My first concert and one of the happiest days of my life!

    Head in The Clouds - Music Festival

    November 2021, Los Angeles

    This was my first music festival! It was held post-Covid but due to safety regulations, most people wore a mask. This festival was a 2-day event and had so many amazing artists at different stages!

    Fun fact: I wrote a 10 page essay while I was here because the assignment was due the day after the festival ended. I also threw up twice inside the pit on Day 2 (I caught the vomit in my mask & recovered shortly after).

    The Artists I saw & have photos of: DPR IAN, DPR LIVE, CL, ILLENIUM, Rich Brian, BIBI, keshi, NIKI, eaJ

    The Driver Era

    June 2023, Los Angeles

    The Driver Era consists of Rocky Lynch and Ross Lynch (they used to be apart of the family band R5).

    SVDDEN DEATH

    July 2023, Los Angeles

    SVDDEN DEATH is a DJ who specializes in heavy dubstep. Special Guest: Marshmello.

    Bittersweet Daze

    July 2023, Los Angeles

    Bittersweet Daze was a concert headlining Cavetown, and featured other artists: mxmtoon, Ricky Montgomery, grentperez.

    NF - HOPE TOUR

    August 2023, Anaheim

    My friend and I impulsively bought tickets to go to NF's concert. It was nice to not have to drive all the way to LA for it!

    Lauv - the between albums tour

    September 2023, Shanghai

    This concert was such a great experience, and also my first concert in Shanghai!

    Fun fact: The concert venue was a 5 minute walk from campus!

    keshi - epilogue tour

    November 2023, Shanghai

    My second concert in Shanghai, my roommate and I had a great time! This was also my second time seeing keshi.

    Fun fact: We had school the next day and were doing homework while waiting for the concert to start.

    Countdown NYE

    December 2023/January 2024, Los Angeles

    Countdown NYE is a rave festival on December 30 and 31st, and lasts until January 1, when the new year starts.

    This was my first rave festival and my friends and I only went on the 31st.

    This event was quite fun but also extremely cold because of the winter.

    Malcolm Todd - Sweet Boy Tour

    May 2024, Los Angeles

    I went to Malcolm Todd's concert in LA right after I arrived home for the summer from Shanghai.

    This was another super fun concert as I got to stand and not sit, so I was fairly close to the stage.

    beabadoobee - This is How Tomorrow Moves Tour

    September 2024, New York City

    beabadoobee! This concert was super impulsive because my friends and I bought tickets the day of, and we were almost late for her set so we were running to the event.

    This was my first concert in New York!
